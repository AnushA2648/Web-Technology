SMVITM Granted Autonomy

Shri Madhwa Vadiraja Institute of Technology and Management, located in Vishwothamanagara, Bantakal, Udupi, has officially attained autonomous status this academic year. This achievement empowers the institution to design its own curriculum and foster innovation in teaching and research.
